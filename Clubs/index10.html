About Us

Nadagaalaya,the Drama Club of KPRIET,focusses on enlightening students about the importance of drama as a Dynamic art form. The mission of the drama club is to use drama to teach people about the power of confidence and creativity. It encourages students to perform drama activities with a view of helping them make critical judgements and understand inferences in a dramatic script which prove to be important in day-to-day communication.
