About Us

The Cooking Club of KPRIET provides hands on cooking experience while learning about kitchen safety and sanitation, recipe preparation, food presentation and nutrition. The mission of KPRIET Food Club is to celebrate different cultures through food. They conduct weekly gatherings that consists of eating various cuisines, playing games, and having fun discussions. The food club also helps us to develop our skills in leadership and team building..
