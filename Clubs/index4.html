About Us

Tamil Mandram KPRIET focusses on enhancing the tamil language skills. It's goal is to spread the magnificence of Tamil language, having been the Oldest Living language on this planet. The club conducts a variety of contests and invites veteran speakers for webinars. It also provides training to interested students for improving their communication thus making them well versed in it.
