About Us

Thulir Club KPRIET, is a club to help the needy. "The greatness of humanity is not in being human, but in being humane", is it's motto. The mission of this club is to show the world that every child regardless of their needs, deserves to have a loving childhood and be treated with dignity and care. Activities conducted by the club motivates the students to help the needy. Thulir Club KPRIET not only conducts such activities, but also visits orphanages with a view of providing them with basic neccessities.
