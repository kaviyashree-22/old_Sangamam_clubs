About Us

Audiofarmers KPRIET Music Club serves as a platform to nurture and showcase the musical talent of the individual. It's mission is to support and encourage the musical talent of the students and to provide a conducive environment for mutual learning. The club focusses on stimulating the creativity, passion and perseverance of the students towards music. It amplifies the positive vibration of the college atmosphere with pleasant music performances. Apart from conducting various events and competitions, the Music Club enhances student’s skill through vocal, guitar, keyboard and drums training to the interested students on Saturdays.
